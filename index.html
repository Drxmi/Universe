A Legacy of Purity & Passion

From the heart of India to the homes of the world.

Founded on generations of artisanal knowledge, Trivedi London embodies the spirit of ancient Indian traditions. We traverse the subcontinent to source the purest resins, the most fragrant botanicals, and the rarest woods. Our mission is to share the timeless art of agarbatti with a global audience, transforming spaces and elevating moments through the power of authentic aroma.
